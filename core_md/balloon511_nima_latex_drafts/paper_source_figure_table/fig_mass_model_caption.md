# Figure: Detector/Cryostat Proxy Mass Model

Two-dimensional views of the baseline detector/cryostat proxy mass model. The top
X--Z overview shows the DR tower, detector bay, active shield, and side-entry
511 keV beam path. The lower detector-bay detail shows the TES absorber stack,
open central beam region, and off-axis Cu thermal links. These are simulation
schematics; circular x-axis parts and side apertures are represented
by MEGAlib-compatible proxy shapes rather than final CAD solids.

Source figures: copied from the current geometry-closure render outputs.
